**KKP Onyx** is a high-performance, desktop-native PDF reader engineered specifically for students, researchers, and professionals who spend extended hours studying digital documents. 

Traditional PDF readers with harsh white backgrounds cause severe visual fatigue and headaches over time. KKP Onyx solves this physical pain point by introducing a native, real-time Dark Mode engine that inverts document colors while preserving image fidelity. Built for extreme reading endurance, it allows users to study longer, retain more information, and drastically reduce eye strain.

## ✨ Core Features

* **Ergonomic Dark Mode Engine:** Real-time color inversion and brightness attenuation designed to combat eye strain during late-night study sessions.
* **Continuous Smart Scroll:** A fluid, edge-less reading experience that eliminates jarring page jumps, maintaining deep cognitive focus and flow.
* **Dual-Page Spread View:** Automatically stitches pages for a traditional textbook-style reading experience, optimizing horizontal desktop monitors.
* **Hardware-Accelerated Rendering:** Powered by PyQt6 and PyMuPDF for zero-lag zooming, smooth panning, and rapid high-resolution document handling.
* **Distraction-Free UI:** A minimalist, modern interface that puts your textbook front and center.
